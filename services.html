FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are my qualifications?

I hold a bachelor of Media Design, majoring in Interactive Design from the Media Design School in Auckland, New Zealand. I specialise in UX, UI design, website design and frontend web development. I am a designer that designs to optimise experience through collaboration, communication and human centered design techniques, ensuring all that I create is both usable and functional and an enjoyable experience for the user intended.

What is my background?

I have always been a curious creative. I love figuring out why things are the way they are but also using this insight to push the boundaries of how I can make improve the usability and functionality of a product or service, while optimising the experience for the user intended.

This curiousity has led me to different paths throughout life, dabbling in psychology, education and statistic papers at The University of Auckland before returning to my creative roots, and using what I had learnt and taking it into the world of Interactive design.

What makes me different?

I am a designer that optimises experience through collaborative design, human centered design, and always thinking about the usability and functionality of a designed product or service to suit the user it is intended for, ensuring positive creative outcomes.
