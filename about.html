About

WHO I AM

Olivia Peterson

I am an Auckland based UX & UI Designer. I have recently graduated from a Bachelor of Media Design, majoring in Interactive Design at The Media Design School & I am excited to start my journey down the design career pathway.
I specialise in UX / UI design, web design & frontend web development. I focus on optimising experience by creating strategic, purposeful designs that are dynamic & are the focal point of modern, accessible & enjoyable user experiences, that most importantly are part of a product or service that addresses an issue users face and provides a effective functioning solution to it.
I was fortunate enough to be apart of The Media Design School Student Executive for the past two years as well as currently being a part of the DINZ Student Council. I am looking forward to continuing to unleash my curiosity in the design space and the problems I am going to solve along the way.
